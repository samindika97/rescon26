International Seminar on Water safety and Security - A global perspective

📅 26th March 2026 (Thursday) ⏰ 9:30 AM 🏛️ JRDC Auditorium 💧 Water Safety · Security · Global Perspective

This international seminar will explore water safety and security from a global perspective, addressing critical challenges and innovative solutions in water management.

Key topics to be covered:

  • Global water security challenges and threats
  • Innovative technologies for water safety
  • Policy frameworks and international cooperation
  • Case studies from around the world

Join us for this insightful seminar led by a renowned expert in the field.

Pre-Conference Workshop 01 Completed

International Workshop on Aquatic Biodiversity Studies by eDNA Metabarcoding

📅 12th March 2026 (Wednesday) ⏰ 8:30 AM onwards 🏛️ PGIS New Conference Room 🐟 Aquatic Biodiversity · eDNA · Metabarcoding

This international workshop will focus on the application of eDNA metabarcoding techniques in studying aquatic biodiversity, providing hands-on training and insights into modern biodiversity assessment methods.

Workshop highlights:

  • Introduction to eDNA metabarcoding technology
  • Sampling and laboratory protocols
  • Data analysis and interpretation
  • Case studies in aquatic biodiversity monitoring

Participants will gain practical skills and knowledge to apply eDNA methods in their research.
